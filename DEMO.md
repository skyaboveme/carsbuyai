### 1. **Landing Page Experience**

### 2. **AI Car Shopping Assistant (Carla)**
**Try these demo conversations:**

```
"I need a reliable family SUV under $35,000 in Seattle"
```
- Carla will ask for your ZIP code
- Returns real car listings with TruePrice analysis
- Each car gets a deal score (1-10)
- Shows estimated market values

```
"Help me negotiate the price on a 2021 Honda CR-V listed at $28,500"
```
- Activates negotiation mode
- Provides professional email templates

### 3. **TruePrice Analysis System**
Every car listing includes:
- **Deal Score**: 1-10 rating (10 = excellent deal)
- **Market Analysis**: "Priced $2,000 below market average"
- **Estimated Value**: AI-calculated fair market price
- **Negotiation Leverage**: How much room for negotiation

### 4. **Finance Calculator**
- Real-time loan calculations
- Adjustable down payment, interest rate, term
- Shows total interest paid
- Monthly payment breakdowns

### 5. **AI Image Generator**
- Generate custom car images
- Describe any car scenario
- Perfect for visualization
- Powered by Google's Imagen model

## 🚀 Live Demo Steps

### Step 1: Start Chat
1. Click "Start Car Shopping with AI"
2. Meet Carla, your AI assistant
3. Try a prompt suggestion or type your own

### Step 2: Search for Cars
```
"Find me a used Toyota Camry under $25,000 in Los Angeles"
```
- Carla will search real listings
- Returns formatted car cards
- Each shows TruePrice analysis

### Step 3: Analyze a Deal
Click "TruePrice Analysis" on any car to see:
- Deal quality score
- Market comparison
- Negotiation advice

### Step 4: Start Negotiation
1. Click "Negotiate" on any car
2. Carla becomes your negotiation agent
3. Get professional email templates
4. Receive counter-strategy advice

### Step 5: Calculate Financing
1. Use the sidebar to access "Finance Calculator"
2. Input car price, down payment, interest rate
3. See real-time monthly payment calculations

### Step 6: Generate Car Images
1. Access "Image Generator" from sidebar
2. Describe your dream car
3. AI generates custom visualization
